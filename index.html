About King City Music

Established in 2013, King City Music is an independent record label based in Sacramento, California. We focus on supporting emerging artists who bring authenticity and innovation to their craft.

Our mission is simple: provide artists with professional support, fair deals, and the infrastructure to grow their careers. From distribution to publishing administration to direct artist support — we handle the business so you can focus on the art.

King City Music is part of the larger King City umbrella brand, with expansion planned into publishing and adjacent services.
